Jinlong Hu, Ph.D.

Introduction

Dr. Jinlong Hu is currently an associate professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at South China University of Technology (SCUT). Before joining School of Computer Science and Engineering in September 2013, he was a senior engineer in Information Network Engineering and Research Center (South China Network Center of CERNET) at SCUT. He joined SCUT in 2004. He was also a faculty member at Guangdong Key Laboratory of Multimodal Big Data Intelligent Analysis (renamed from Guangdong Key Laboratory of Communication and Computer Networks in 2024) at SCUT since 2004. He was a visiting scholar at Pennsylvania State University from 2016 to 2017.

Research Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence (especially Machine Learning, eXplainable Artificial Intelligence)
  • Brain Imaging Analysis for Brain Science and Brain Disorders Using Artificial Intelligence
  • Computer Networking and Security, High Performance Computing and Cloud Computing
